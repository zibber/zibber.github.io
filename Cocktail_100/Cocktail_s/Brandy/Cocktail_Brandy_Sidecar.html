About Sidecar

The Sidecar is a classic cocktail that some claim was invented at the Ritz Hotel in Paris, France, during World War I. According to drink historians, a captain staying at Ritz was often driven to the hotel in the sidecar of a motorcycle. He would arrive cold after being in the sidecar at night, so he requested a pre-dinner drink that would warm him up-a drink that became know as the Sidecar.

Key Ingediants
  Brandy 1 1/2 oz.
  Triple Sec 1 1/2 oz.
  Lemon Juice 1/2 oz.
  Sugar X oz.
Time Required
Total: 3 min
Preperation: 2 min
Mixing: 1 min
Instructions
.01 Apply sugar to a wet cocktail glass rim.
.02 Combine brandy, triple sec, and lemon juice in a cocktail shaker.
.03 Shake with ice.
.04 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
