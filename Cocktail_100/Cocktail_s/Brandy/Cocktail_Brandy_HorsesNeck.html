About Horse's Neck

While the original recipe was without alcohol, in the early 1920s brandy was added to make the Horse's Neck a truly refreshing cocktail. The Lemon peel is used to resemble the neck of a horse dipping its head into the drink.

Key Ingediants
  Brandy 2 oz.
  Ginger Ale 5 oz.
  Bitters 2 dashes
  Lemon X oz.
Time Required
Total: 3 min
Preperation: 2 min
Mixing: 1 min
Instructions
.01 Pour brandy, ginger ale, and bitters (2 dashes) into a highball glass with ice.
.02 Stir well.
.03 Garnish with an lemon twist.
