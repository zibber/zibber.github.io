About Between The Sheets
Between the Sheets is a strong cocktail that is said to have originated in Paris during the early 1930s. It is the variation of the Sidecar. Try substituting brandy with peach schnapps for a fruitier alternative.
Key Ingediants
  Brandy 1 oz.
  Light Rum 1 oz.
  Triple Sec 1 oz.
  Lemon Juice 1 oz.
  Lemon 1 oz.
Time Required
Total: 3 min
Preperation: 2 min
Mixing: 1 min
Instructions
.01 Combine branky, light rum, triple sec, and lemon juice in a cocktail shaker.
.02 Shake with ice.
.03 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
.04 Garnish with an lemon twist.
