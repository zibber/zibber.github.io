About Jack Rose

While there are a number of possible origins behind the Jack Rose, many claim the drink gets its name from the combination of “applejack” (another name for apple brandy) and the “rose” color of the drink caused by the addition of grenadine.

Key Ingediants
  Brandy 1/2 oz.
  Grenadine 1/2 oz.
  Lime Juice 1 oz.
Time Required
Total: 2 min
Preperation: 1 min
Mixing: 1 min
Instructions
.01 Combine branky, grenadine and lime juice in a cocktail shaker.
.02 Shake with ice.
.03 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
