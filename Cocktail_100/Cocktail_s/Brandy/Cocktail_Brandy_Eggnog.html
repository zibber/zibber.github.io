About Eggnog
A staple for winter holiday gatherings and parties, Eggnog is a smooth and sweet delight, pairing well with cookies or other treats. Try substituting brandy with whiskey for a slightly edgier taste.
Key Ingediants
  Brandy 1 1/2 oz.
  Eggnog 5 oz.
  Ground Nutmeg X oz.
Time Required
Total: 2 min
Preperation: 1 min
Mixing: 1 min
Instructions
.01 Pour brandy and eggnog into a mug
.02 Stir well.
.03 Garnish with a sprinkling of nutmeg.
