About Champagne Cocktail

This drink takes its roots from a New York cocktail competition in 1889. The gold medal was awarded to bartender John Doughty based on a drink he called "Business Brace". This drink is said to be the predecessor of the Champagne Cocktail

Key Ingediants
  Champagne 5 oz.
  Brandy 1 oz.
  Angostura Bitter 2 dashes
  Sugar X oz.
Time Required
Total: 2 min
Preperation: 1 min
Mixing: 1 min
Instructions
.01 Place a sugar cube in a Champagne flute.
.02 Soak the sugar cube with brandy and Angostura bitters (2 dashes).
.03 Top with Champagne.
