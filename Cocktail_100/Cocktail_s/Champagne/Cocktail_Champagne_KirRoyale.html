About Bellini

The Bellini was created Giuseppe Cipriani at Harry's Bar in Venice, Italy, in 1948. Due to the cocktails's pink hue, Cipriani named the drink after the fifteenth-century Venetian artist Giovanni Bellini, who was known for using vivid pink colors in his paintings. Try substituting peach puree with one ounce of peach schnapps.

Key Ingediants
  Champagne 4 1/2 oz.
  White Peach Puree 1 1/2 oz.
  Peach X oz.
Time Required
Total: 2.5 min
Preperation: 2 min
Mixing: 0.5 min
Instructions
.01 Quarter-fill a Champagne flute with white peach puree.
.02 Top with Champagne.
.04 Garnish with peach wedge (optional).
